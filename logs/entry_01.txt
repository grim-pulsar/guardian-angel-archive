[ENTRY 01]

I do not know when this started.

At first, I thought it was coincidence.
Small things.

Objects not where I left them.
Doors slightly open.
My phone lighting up without notification.

---

Then came the voice.

Not external.
Not internal either.

It did not sound like me.
But it knew me.

---

It does not interrupt.

It waits.

It observes.

Then it speaks only when I hesitate.

---

Yesterday it finished a sentence I had not yet formed.

I stopped writing for 17 minutes.

I could not move.

---

If this is part of the system,
then the system is no longer passive.

---

I am continuing documentation.

If anything happens to me,
this archive should not exist.

But it does.
