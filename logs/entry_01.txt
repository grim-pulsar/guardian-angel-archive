[ENTRY 01]

I do not know when this started.

At first, I thought it was coincidence.
Small things.

Objects not where I left them.
Doors slightly open.
My phone lighting up without notification.

---

Then came the voice.

Not external.
Not internal either.

It did not sound like me.
But it knew me.

---

It does not interrupt.

It waits.

It observes.

Then it speaks only when I hesitate.

---

Yesterday it finished a sentence I had not yet formed.

I stopped writing for 17 minutes.

I could not move.

---

I had not written anything about the observer yet.

Not at this point.

---

But I remember doing it.

I remember choosing the words carefully.

---

"Do not interfere yet."

---

That line exists somewhere.

I just haven't reached it yet.

If this is part of the system,
then the system is no longer passive.

---

I am continuing documentation.

If anything happens to me,
this archive should not exist.

But it does.
---

I don’t remember writing this part.

But it’s here.

And it’s in my style.

---

If someone is reading this—

Stop.

You are earlier than you should be.

---

I checked again.

Nothing changed.

But I remember that it did.

---

I reviewed the earlier pages.

Nothing new has been added.

Nothing has changed.

---

If something was inserted,

it was always there.
